# Capstone

The curriculum culminates in a multi-year capstone rather than a thesis.

The capstone would require designing and implementing an integrated ecosystem that demonstrates:

* A multilingual programming environment (Hindawi principles).
* A computational linguistic participation framework (ILM).
* A learning and schema-generation platform (PEDLER/AyeLearn concepts).
* A competency and evidence architecture (ValidCV concepts).
* A distributed execution substrate (AyeVDI concepts).
* A symbolic representation and transformation framework (AyeHandsum concepts).
* A connected healthcare platform integrating HIE, EHR/EMR, consent, AI, and medical devices.
* AI agents orchestrated over a knowledge graph rather than isolated models.
* Alignment with international interoperability and competency standards where applicable.

In academic terms, this is not equivalent to a single MSc or PhD. It is closer to the curriculum of a multidisciplinary research institute, where the objective is not mastery of one field, but the synthesis of multiple mature disciplines into a coherent architectural framework.
