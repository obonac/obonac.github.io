## Notes

  under the same terms.
- **Software — GPL-3.0-or-later.** Copyleft; distributed source stays open. SPDX
  headers apply per file.

  can be reused without friction.
- **Methods & inventions — proprietary.** Where a method is genuinely novel it may be
  patentable and is offered under separate commercial terms. **AyeAI** provides
  consulting on patent strategy and filing. Openly publishing the surrounding tooling,
  schemas and documentation (above) is compatible with reserving the patentable core.

## Precedence

Where the blanket "all rights reserved" notice and a specific open licence appear to
conflict for a given file, **the specific open licence for that file's category
governs that file**. The reserved-rights notice covers everything not so granted —
chiefly the patentable methods.

## Why open where we can

A gated, openly published artifact is independently checkable, timestamped
(OpenTimestamps), and citable by DOI. That compounds into credibility, reuse and
trust in a way a closed artifact cannot — so the default here is *open for content,
code and metadata; reserved only for the patentable core*.
